About me

I am an engineer from the École des Ponts ParisTech. I am currently working as a PhD student at Inria Paris, affiliated with the MATHERIAL team, and at École des Ponts ParisTech inthe CERMICS laboratory. My PhD advisors are Claude Le Bris and Frédéric Legoll. My reseach is founded by the FSMP.
